Quite often, a sequence needs to be repeated for a set number of times.

Can you get this sequence to make sense?

pump in waterrepeat 72000times:rotate washing machine drumthen pump out waterpower off

Fantastic! Count-controlled loops come in handy when you know exactly how often the sequence has to be repeated.

Psst: this sequence describes a washing machine. It pumps in water, rotates the drum about 72,000 times, then pumps out water and powers off.
