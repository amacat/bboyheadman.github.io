There are two records in our table that have the same city field. What are they?

SELECT first_name FROM people
WHERE city IN (
   SELECT city FROM people
   GROUP BY city
   HAVING COUNTNUM(city) > 1 );
 first_name------------ Sherlock   Ada       

Brilliant! We've used a sub query that looks for a city that occurs more than once, and asked to see the first names of the records that have that city.
