Let's get back to you blog, for which hyperlinks will be a huge upgrade.

Insert a horizontal rule at the bottom of the web page and add links to your social profiles that open the web pages in a new tab or window.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <head>
  <title>My blog</title>
 </head>
 <body>
  <!-- Things from earlier on -->
  <hr>
  <ahref="https://fb.com/hgllnt"target="_blank">Add me</a>
  <ahref="https://twitter.com/hgllnt"target="_blank">Follow me</a>
 </body>
</html>
<html>
<head>
<title>My blog</title>
</head>
<body><h1>Welcome</h1><p>I'm <strong>Henry</strong> and this is a blog about <em>my life</em>.</p><hr><a href="https://fb.com/hgllnt" target="_blank">Add me</a>
  <a href="https://twitter.com/hgllnt" target="_blank">Follow me</a></body>
</html>
]]>

Great! Now that your blog has links, it's connected with other web pages.
