Now, let's spice up your blog with some of the things we just talked about.

Divide your introduction into a big heading and a paragraph. Mark your name as important and emphasize what the blog is about.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <head>
  <title>My blog</title>
 </head>
 <body>
  <h1>Welcome</h1>
  <p>I'm <strong>Henry</strong> and this is a blog about <em>my life</em>.</p>
 </body>
</html>
